SINIGANG

Pork Sinigang

Pork Sinigang is a delicious Filipino sour soup dish. The soup is made from any cut of pork along tomato, string beans, spinach, and tamarind.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Pork
  • 10 Strings of Kangkong leaves
  • 2 pcs Tomato
  • 2 pcs Onions
  • 2 pcs Long Green Pepper
  • 40g Sinigang Mix

Steps

  1. Boiled 3 cups of water with the Tomato and Onions. Put in the pork meat and Boiled up to 40 mins or until meat is tender.
  2. Put the Sinigang Mix and boiled up to 5 mins.
  3. Put the Kangkong leaves and long green pepper. Cover and turn the heat off. Let the residual heat cook the leaves and long green pepper for 3 mins.
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
